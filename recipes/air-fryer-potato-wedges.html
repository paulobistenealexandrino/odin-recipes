Air Fryer Potato Wedges

Home


Source: allrecipes.com

Description

Deliciously crispy air-fried potato wedges are perfectly seasoned and ready to serve in just over 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • 1 and a half tablespoons olive oil
  • Half teaspoon ground paprika
  • Half teaspoon parsley flakes
  • Half teaspoon chili powder
  • Half teaspoon sea salt
  • One eighth ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat an air fryer to 400ยบ F (200ยบ C).
  2. Cut each potato in eighth parts lengthwise. Gather the seasonings.
  3. Place potato wedges in a large bowl. Add olive oil, paprika, parsley, chili, salt, and pepper; mix until well combined.
  4. Place half of the potato wedges in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Flip wedges with tongs and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Remove to a plate. Repeat to cook the remaining wedges.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
