Brazilian White Rice

Home


Source: allrecipes.com (adapted)

Description

White rice is the main side dish in Brazilian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of long-grain white rice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan
  2. Stir in the garlic and cook until it gets golden brown
  3. Add the rice, cook and stir it until it begins to brown
  4. Pour a little more water then enough to cover the rice
  5. Add salt at your taste. Usually, the water should start resemble seawater
  6. When the water boils, reduce the fire to low
  7. Cover the saucepan allowing it to simmer until the water has been absorbed
  8. If the rice is still uncooked, add just a tiny bit more water and let it be absorbed
  9. After cook it should be loose, not stick
  10. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving
